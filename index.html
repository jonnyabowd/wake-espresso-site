Wake Espresso was born from a simple idea between two brothers: that great coffee should feel like home. What started as a passion project is growing into a gathering place for the community — a spot where every cup is made with care and every visit feels familiar. More than a coffee shop, it's a space built on heart, hard work, and a love for our neighbors here in Canyon Lake.
