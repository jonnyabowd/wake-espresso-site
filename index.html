Locally Brewed, Lake Approved

Founded by Canyon Lake natives, Wake Espresso is all about great coffee and lake vibes.
Whether you're heading out on the water or just passing through, grab a handcrafted coffee, tea, or espresso drink from our convenient drive-thru or walk-up window.
